Security Services

We are operating our security services as per PSARA Act 2005. We have MOU with PSARA approved cum registered training centre to provide training as per the guidelines of PSARA 2005.
We have our own training team &infrastructural setup to provide On Job training (OJT)/Refresher courses related to FIRE, FIRST-AID & SAFETY, new threats, security environment orientation, new working area, client requirements and specific needs.
A formal training capsule of 7 days is imparted to the security staff covering the essential duty related subjects.

What Brings Our Clients Here?

Multi-Purpose, Multi Skilled and Multi Clientele Manpower

Well trained & skilled Manpower is our core strength. We ensure customised manpower services along with security and much needed peace of mind to the clients. Our philosophy is- right candidate, at the right place, for the right duty under the right man. We are very particular about the job oriented training due to particularities of each site. Random monitoring is our priority. We maintain a very formal roaster to ensure this important aspect.

Recruitment, Training and Discipline

Our recruitment process is very stringent, elaborate & time tested. Final recruitment is preceded thorough scrutiny of all documents like proof of DOB, voter card/UID, relevant references, qualification,salary structureand residential address, verification by the police & through our company process, past experience and physical standards. We have zero tolerance for indiscipline. Our appointed staff works as a single cohesive unit with no unionism. We strive to project as a trained team. We invite corporate experts, communication and specifically needed trainers for their advice and sharing of experience and case studies with our team.

Deployment

Our initial focus after deployment remains on proper orientation, observation & acquaintance. After deployment for a few days, a detailed in house appraisal is carried out listing out the security related observations, functioning of the security lights, locks & keys, leakage, gaps in the security apparatus, needed reduction/ addition in the security staff, plugging of certain entry/exit points and practised/ existing stranger/ public movement especially after the last light.

Training & Development

Training is an ongoing process and it can never be called 100% complete or fool proof. New manpower, new development, new business, new opportunities and new locations pose us different challenges and threats. Accordingly, we are always on our toes to cater for all such existing and potential exigencies. Use of technology and educated manpower and surging expectations of the clients only add to our resolve and focus.
We provide onsite job training to our employees regularly. The training is imparted on various skill sets such as allocated duties, acting in the absence of orders, protection of manpower, info and property, safety and security, first aid, alarm system, quick calling to police, fire brigade, management, medical resources, dressing, courteous behaviour, documentation, frisking, accounting of documents, articles, baggage management/ handling, identification of strangers, etiquettes &behaviour and Personal grooming. Use of chemicals & machines, safety gears and protections too are its essential components

Detailed Reports

This is followed by a detailed report to the concerned staff of the site for their consideration for needed further remedial actions and improvement.
We submit periodic reports indicating areas requiring review. In cases of urgency, a spot call is made for prompt action.
